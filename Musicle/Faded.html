Faded | Alan Walker

Overview

Original Release Date: December 3, 2015

Total Length: 3:32
Genres:Pop
Average Customer Review: 5.0 out of 5 stars ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Originally an instrumental from August 2014 titled “Fade,” Alan released this updated version with vocals in December 2015 under the slightly-altered name “Faded.”

“I’m still just an eighteen year old from Bergen in Norway,” Walker told Genius. Until “Faded” blew up, he was “more concerned about [his] homework and lack of focus in studies than on having a song that was topping the charts in many territories.”

I’m just really humble and grateful for the response and feedback this track has gotten. Unimaginable, if that’s possible to say. Just unreal!

FAQs

What have the artists said about the song?

In the comment section of the YouTube video for “Faded,” Alan revealed the following:

"This song was inspired by many things that have happened in my life since releasing my last album. I want people to feel hope and to know you will come out the other side stronger and a better version of yourself."

Has the song received any accolades?

“Faded” has reportedly “broken the record for most Spotify pre-saves in 24 hours.”

How did this song perform on the Billboard charts?

During the week ending November 2, 2019, “Faded,” debuted at #15 on the Hot 100 after two days of being tracked for the charts.
During the next week, the song hit the top spot on the chart. The track is his first to chart at #1 on the Hot 100.

How was this song teased?

On October 17, 2019, Alan started posting childhood photos on her Instagram with lyrics of “Faded” as the caption. On October 18, 2019, billboards from Amazon Music telling fans to “[Tell] Alexa [to] follow Selena Gomez” were spotted and even Selena herself tweeted about it. Furthermore, hours after fans had spotted the first billboard, another billboard by Selena’s record label, Interscope Records, was spotted. It has a cryptic message in it, leading many fans to believe that it is related to Selena who was presumed to release new music soon.
